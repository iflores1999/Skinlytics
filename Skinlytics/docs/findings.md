# Skinlytics Findings

## Brand Analysis

### Business Question
Which skincare brands have the highest average customer ratings?

### Result
Brands such as MARA, Hourglass, and StriVectin ranked among the highest-rated skincare brands after filtering for brands with at least 10 products.

### Business Insight
Brands with consistently high customer ratings may represent strong partnership or promotional opportunities for beauty retailers.

## Pricing Analysis

### Business Question
Do more expensive skincare products receive higher customer ratings?

### Result

| Price Range | Average Rating | Number of Products |
|-------------|---------------:|-------------------:|
| Under $25 | 4.12 | 528 |
| $25–$50 | 4.25 | 904 |
| $50–$100 | 4.30 | 728 |
| Over $100 | 4.20 | 259 |

### Business Insight
Products priced between **$50 and $100** received the highest average customer rating (4.30).

Products priced **over $100** did not receive the highest ratings, suggesting that higher prices do not necessarily result in greater customer satisfaction.

The **$25–$50** price range contained the largest number of products (904), making it the most common pricing category in the dataset.
