# Skinlytics – Project Overview

## Project Goal

Skinlytics is a data analytics project I created to analyze skincare product data and turn it into useful business insights. The project uses Python, SQL, and Tableau to clean, organize, analyze, and visualize product data.

Instead of reviewing thousands of products one by one, Skinlytics helps identify patterns in customer ratings, product popularity, pricing, ingredients, and skincare categories.

## Dataset

This project uses a publicly available Sephora product dataset from Kaggle.

The dataset includes information such as:

- Product names
- Brand names
- Customer ratings
- Number of reviews
- Product prices
- Ingredients
- Product categories

The dataset was used for educational and portfolio purposes.

## Business Problem

Sephora offers thousands of products across many brands and skincare categories. Reviewing this information manually would be time-consuming and would make it difficult to identify trends.

Skinlytics helps solve this problem by organizing and analyzing the data so business teams can better understand customer preferences, product performance, and pricing patterns.

## Business Questions

This project answers the following business questions:

- Which skincare brands have the highest average customer ratings?
- Which skincare products have the highest number of customer reviews?
- Is there a relationship between product price and customer ratings?
- Which ingredients appear most frequently in skincare products?
- Which skincare categories receive the highest customer satisfaction?

## Project Workflow

The project follows an end-to-end data analytics process:

1. Import the raw Sephora product dataset from Kaggle.
2. Explore the data using Python and Pandas.
3. Clean missing, duplicated, and inconsistent values.
4. Load the cleaned data into a SQLite database.
5. Write SQL queries to answer business questions.
6. Create Tableau visualizations and a dashboard.
7. Document the findings and publish the project on GitHub.

## Dashboard Visualizations

The Tableau dashboard includes:

- Top 10 Highest-Rated Skincare Brands with at Least 10 Products
- Most Reviewed Skincare Products
- Average Customer Rating by Price Range

These visualizations make it easier to compare product performance and identify customer trends.

## Expected Outcome

The goal of Skinlytics is to transform raw Sephora skincare data into clear and useful business insights.

The completed project demonstrates practical skills in:
